Understanding Yield Prediction

Our yield prediction model uses machine learning to estimate crop yields based on various environmental and agricultural factors. For best results, please provide accurate measurements.

  • Select the specific crop type you're growing
  • Enter the total cultivation area in hectares
  • Provide seasonal rainfall data in millimeters
  • Include average temperature during the growing season
  • Specify the amount of fertilizer and pesticide used
